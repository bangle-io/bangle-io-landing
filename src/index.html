Note taking for the next decade

Bangle.io is a powerful yet simple note taking web app that works on top of your locally saved Markdown files.

Is your data held hostage?

Bangle.io saves everything in a folder of your choice in your computer. Nothing every leaves your computer, this is fundamentally safer than any amount of encryption other products put in their servers.
