FAQ

Why should I use Bangle.io?

If any of the following strike your interest then you should give Bangle.io a try:
  • You want to keep your notes on your computer under your control.
  • You like Notion / Google doc style WYSIWYG UI interface.
  • You find split screen and multi-tabs interface helpful while taking notes.
  • You have Markdown notes lying around.
  • You care about powerful note taking features.
  • You want to support a small indie project.

Where are my notes stored?

Bangle.io saves your notes in a folder of your choice in your computer. Bangle.io does not track or analyze you or your data. For more information, please read our privacy policy.

How do I backups my notes?

Bangle.io leaves this up to you! Notes under Bangle.io are just any other file, so backup them along with rest of your other important file. That said, we recommend using git for a version controlled backup.

Is there a desktop app?

Yes, you can visit app.bangle.io and click on top right install button to install it as a PWA.

Does Bangle.io work offline?

Yes, Bangle.io works offline.

What is the browser requirement?

Bangle.io currently works in Chrome or Edge because they support Native File Storage API. If you cannot use these browsers, we recommend downloading the app.

Is Bangle.io free?

Yes, Bangle.io is free and we plan to keep the main product free. For a sustainable future, we can consider providing addon services to financially support the development for those who can and want to.
